People have all sort of hobbies; people collect all sorts of things. My name is Paula and I collect stories. The stories vary - like driving through dirt roads in middle-of-nowhere Cambodia to eating a snake to jumping off a building in Bolivia. I have this desire to always keep going and to see everything. That's why I created Black Journal. It's an online journal filled with my stories, naturally. However, I'd like to inspire anyone who needs a little nudge to get creative and do something.

I've been to 4 continents, 9 islands, 25 countries and 2 terretories. My favourite place is Luxembourg, Luxemburg. I love eating streetfood. My hope is to spend a month at a Muay Thai camp in Thailand one of these days.
